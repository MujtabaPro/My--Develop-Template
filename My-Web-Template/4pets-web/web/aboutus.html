Our Story

Since our beginning in 1997, we’ve been dedicated to making life easier for pet parents. It all started with the launch of PetFoodDirect.com – the first online pet store to deliver pet food and supplies right to your front door. Over time, we realized there was so much more we could be doing to help our customers navigate the incredible journey that is pet parenting!

Today, our network is made up of more than 30 of the top pet websites and reaches 12+ million pet parents every month. We’ve expanded our family of brands to include Pet360.com – the #1 resource for pet parents; petMD.com – the largest online library of pet health information; BlogPaws – the largest professional network of pet bloggers and social media enthusiasts; and Only Natural Pet – a complete line of all natural pet supplies. Across our network, we provide pet parents places to engage and connect, access trusted advice and expert information and purchase the very best products for their pets.

Our mission is to help you find what you need quickly and affordably, so you can spend more time loving your pets!
